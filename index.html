Services

Exquisite Experiences for Your Cherished Stone

Day Care

Indulge your beloved rock in our premium day care service, where we offer a sanctuary of relaxation and rejuvenation. In our state-of-the-art facility, your pet rock will experience unparalleled care, ensuring they remain at their finest and most radiant condition.

Erosion Natural Detox

Nature has its way of restoring and revitalizing, and with our Erosion Natural Detox, your stone will be treated to a therapeutic session from the wonders of nature. This service gently erodes away any impurities, rejuvenating your rock and restoring its natural luster and brilliance.

Care Box

Elegantly curated with love and precision, our Care Box is the epitome of luxury for your pet stone. Filled with an array of premium products and treatments, this box promises to cater to every need of your rock, ensuring it feels pampered, cherished, and truly special.

Rock Skipping Retreat

Dive into the timeless art of stone skimming with our Rock Skipping Retreat. In serene settings, your stone will glide gracefully across water, guided by our skimming experts. Experience the blend of earth and water in a dance of perfect ripples. It's more than a skip; it's pure elegance in motion.

Rock Therapy

Every stone bears an intricate tale of geologic journeys and age-old memories. Our Stone Therapy Soiree provides a sanctuary for your treasured rock to gracefully share its tales. Guided by our connoisseurs, it'll experience a voyage of introspection, embracing the profound beauty of its existence. A resplendent reprieve for the discerning rock.

Lava Rock Rejuvenation

Immerse your stone in the transformative warmth of our Lava Rock Rejuvenation session. This experience harnesses the earth's fiery energies, allowing your rock to bask in the gentle heat and absorb volcanic vigor. It's a revitalizing journey, reminiscent of the rock's fiery origins and the planet's powerful core.
